# Demo Script

## 60-90 Second Flow

1. Open the Streamlit page. Explain that this is a QQ音乐 embedded AI interaction layer, not a standalone music app.
2. Click play. The page enters a restrained listening state, with the poster behind the central lyric.
3. Let the lyrics scroll for a few seconds. Point out that the current lyric is central, adjacent lines are layered and softened.
4. When the AI high-energy label appears, click the lyric.
5. Explain the three feedback phases: press and charge, lyric expansion, particle release.
6. Click another high-energy marker on the progress bar to show direct timestamp interaction.
7. Click `演示结算`. The player fades into the golden slot-style score settlement.
8. Click `查看本曲共振场`.
9. Click a bright lyric in the cloud. Comments emerge through an electric wave and remain readable below.
10. Click `为这句歌词充能`. Explain that this local state simulates a lyric entering future high-resonance recommendation candidates.

## Key Talking Points

- AI is productized inside playback, lyrics, comments, and sharing logic.
- The demo uses real browser audio playback and Web Audio energy reading.
- Music structure, semantic comments, and final scores are simulated data for demo stability.
- The visual language borrows Tencent Music Hackathon green/gold brand feeling without copying the reference template.
